Our Agenda

India faces a deep-rooted challenge: waste mismanagement and unsanitary public spaces. According to the World Bank, India generates over 62 million tonnes of waste every year, and a large portion of it ends up on streets, drains, and open areas, posing severe health and environmental risks. This portal was created to change that. Our agenda is simple yet powerful: to make every citizen a participant in India's cleanliness revolution. Every report made here is a step toward a cleaner, healthier, and more responsible India. Our mission is to bridge the gap between citizens and authorities, ensuring that sanitation problems are seen, tracked, and solved efficiently. We believe that change begins with awareness and action.
